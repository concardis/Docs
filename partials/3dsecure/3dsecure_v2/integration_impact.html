This page describes the potential impact for 3DS 2.0 for integrators based on the integration type and use case.



Tokenization & Payment: One Session



This section will describe the impact for integration where the tokenization of credit card credentials and a corresponding payment will be handled within the same session.


All actions will be cardholder initiated in this case.


More details about the use cases related to credentials-on-file can be found here.


! Important
For simplicity the recurring scheduled and recurring unscheduled use cases are covered together under just "recurring" as the impact for potential changes will be the same for both.

This section will describe the impact for integration where the tokenization of credit card credentials and a corresponding payment will be handled within the separate sessions.


While all intial transactions will be cardholder initiated the subequent transactions can be cardholder or merchant initiated, depending on the use case.


More details about the use cases related to credentials-on-file can be found here.

For simplicity the recurring scheduled and recurring unscheduled use cases are covered together under just "recurring" as the impact for potential changes will be the same for both.
