In Payment Instruments section you can see details about your Payment Instruments
  • Navigation to Payment Instruments
  • Create Payment Instrument
  • Payment Instruments details
  • Delete Payent Instruments
 

Navigation

Payment Instruments information can be found in "Payment Instruments" menu item. After you click on menu item you must see Payment Instruments list.
 

Create

To create customer, you must click on "Plus" button on top right corner: It will open new window:
1 You can select the payment method in the first field. You can find more information about payment methods here.
2 You can add your custom ID in the "Merchant Payment Instrument Id" field.
3 Add the credit card information here.

Details

You can see detailed Payment Instrument information if you click on "Actions" button for selected Payment Instrument:
It open new page with detailed Payment Instruments information:

Delete

If you want to delete Payment Instrument you must select "Delete Payment Instrument" button on the top right corner:
You will see modal window with for confirmation of deleting: After you confirm, the Payment Instrument will be deleted from the Payment Instruments list.
