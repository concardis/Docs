Create new Basket Item

To create new basket item you first must select "Add Item" button It will open new section for adding basket item details:
      • Name (Required) - Name of the basket item
      • Article number (Required) - Article number
      • Unit price (Required) - Price for one unit
      • Quantity (Required) - Quantity
      • Tax (Required) - Tax in percents
      • Discount  - Discount in Euro
      • Total price (disabled for edit)- Total price without Tax
      • Unit Price With Tax (disabled for edit)- Unit Price With Tax
      • Total Price With Tax (disabled for edit)- Total Price With Tax
After you fill required fields, You will see calculated prices: To save the item click on save button: You will see just saved item information If you want to add another item click on "Add Item" button again
 

Edit Basket Item

To edit basket item you must select edit icon for selected item It will open new section for editing basket item details:
      • Name (Required) - Name of the basket item
      • Article number (Required) - Article number
      • Unit price (Required) - Price for one unit
      • Quantity (Required) - Quantity
      • Tax (Required) - Tax in percents
      • Discount  - Discount in Euro
      • Total price (disabled for edit)- Total price without Tax
      • Unit Price With Tax (disabled for edit)- Unit Price With Tax
      • Total Price With Tax (disabled for edit)- Total Price With Tax
After you edit needed fields, you will see recalculated prices: To save edited item click on save button: You will see just saved item information  
 

Delete Basket Item

  To delete basket Item you must select delete icon for selected basket When you click it, basket item will disappear from items list  
 

 Basket Details in Order transactions

  In Order view you can find basket details in transactions details section: If you click on the arrow of the basket item you can see additional detals
  • Name - Name of the basket item
  • Article number  - Article number
  • Unit price  - Price for one unit without tax
  • Quantity  - Quantity
  • Tax - Tax in percents
  • Total price- Total price without Tax
  • Unit Price With Tax - Unit Price With Tax
  • Total Price With Tax- Total Price With Tax
 
 

 Capture basket Items

  On Capture transaction, merchant can capture all or partial items from basket When you are on Capture screen you will see basket section:   If you click on the arrow of the basket item you can see additional detals It will open full details about the selected basket item:
  • Name - Name of the basket item
  • Article number  - Article number
  • Unit price  - Price for one unit without tax
  • Quantity  - Quantity
  • Tax - Tax in percents
  • Total price- Total price without Tax
  • Unit Price With Tax - Unit Price With Tax
  • Total Price With Tax- Total Price With Tax
To Capture basket items you must check on "Capture" checkbox for selected item It will enable selection of Quantity and calculate Amount to capture: Important! - By default It will capture all units of selected item. If you want partial capture you can change wanted quantity. If you don't want to capture all units, you can change quantity: When you change quantity, the Amount to capture will be recalculated: Now you can continue with capturing wanted amount, by selecting capture button: Now you can see successfully captured items in Transaction details section:
